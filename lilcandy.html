Lil Candypaint


Lil Candy Paint is an American rapper from Atlanta and Chicago. Some of his most well-known songs include "Seventh Sense," "Hop Out," and "Stunt"Lil Candy Paint was born in Chicago and spent his childhood moving back and forth between Atlanta and Chicago.​ He got his name by combining him and his father's love for candy and appreciation of cars into a single moniker. He lists Chief Keef​ as one of his biggest influences
Based out of Savannah GA and the partner-in-crime of Duwap Kaine, Lil Candy Paint has been rapping for a small amount of time but, still has a buzz to his name . He is associated with artists like Duwap Kaine, Warhol.ss, K$UPREME, and SLIMESITO.
He is an up and coming hip-hop recording artist who is recognized for his affiliation with Duwap Kaine. In early 2018, he released a mixtape titled Revenge On My Enemies. He took his stage name from his father's love for candy and appreciation of cars.
Lil Candypaint burst onto people’s radar recently with a stellar new video for “Internet Thug,” and is back on our pages to premiere some new sounds today. Featuring Never Been Average frontman LAMONT, “No Regrets” is a gritty, uptempo banger that shows off Candypaint and LAMONT’s unique sounds and styles, ultimately meshing for a knockout record.
