Comethazine


Comethazine, born July 6th, 1998, is a rapper repping St. Louis and Illinois. Comethazine started rapping in his basement a few years prior to his E.P., Aloe Vera, dropping on iTunes in August of 2016. After dropping the E.P., he gained traction with the songs, “Run It”, and “Hella Choppers”.
Comethazine has stated in interviews that his influences include 50 Cent, Chief Keef, and Rick James. He is currently signed to Todd Moscowitz Alamo Records along with Smokepurpp and Wifisfuneral.
Comethazine has been gaining momentum lately with songs like "Piped Up" and he recently signed a deal with Alamo Records.
