JUICE WRLD


Jared Higgins (born December 2, 1998), otherwise known as Juice WRLD, is an American rapper from Calumet Park, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago, Illinois. Juice was first signed to Internet Money Records© then subsequently signed to Interscope on March 13, 2018. His main producers are Nick Mira and Sidepce.
According to sources, it is worth more than $3 million and would give the rapper from the Chicago suburb of Calumet Park, Illinois, a joint venture with the label. Now just 19, Juice began gaining attention on Soundcloud early on, where he has been posting music since 2015. Last year, his Juice WRLD 999 EP really began racking up streams with the track "Lucid Dreams (Forget Me)" produced by Nick Mira topping 1 million listens.
Juice first gained major popularity from his track “All Girls Are The Same”, which gained even more recognition when popular director Cole Bennett made a music video for it. He’s often compared to Post Malone for his similar sounding voice and style.
Juice WRLD has said on an interview with No Jumper, who has interviewed many up and coming artists before they blow up like Lil Yachty, xxxtentacion, Smokepurpp, Famous Dex, and Lil Xan. He said “that his favorite artist is Chief Keef and his favorite album is Kanye West’s 808s & Heartbreaks.”
